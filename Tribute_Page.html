Linkin Park

One of the best bands of the 2000s

A little history and main albums

  • The creation of the band and the first album - Linkin Park is an American rock band formed in 1996 in Agoura Hills, California. The band consists of Chester Bennington and Mike Shinoda (vocals), Joseph Hahn (Dj), Rob Bourdon (drums), Brad Delson (guitar) and Dave Farrel (bass). Linkin Park released their debut album “Hibrid Theory” in 2000, which became the best-selling album of 2001. It received a Grammy for Best Performance, as well as nominations for Best Rock Album and Best Breakthrough.
  • The Second Album - The second album “Metera” was ranked at the top of the Billboard 200 list. In 2003 the band Linkin Park was named by MTV2 the sixth biggest band of the music video era and also the third best band, behind Oasis and Coldplay.
  • The Third Album - The third studio album "Minutes to Midnight", reached the top of the charts and was the best selling album in 2007. In 2010, a band released "The Tousand Suns", which soon became the best seller in more than fifteen countries. In 2014, the band Linkin Park released “The Hunting Party”, which brings together all the elements and genres of previous works.
